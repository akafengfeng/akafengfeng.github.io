Short bio: I am a Ph.D. candidate majoring in Computer Science at the University at Buffalo (SUNY). My advisor is Prof. Hongxin Hu. I received my bachelor's degree from Xi'an Jiaotong University. I am grateful to have received support for my research from the Amazon Research Award.

Research Interests

My research interests center around cyberscurity and machine learning. I am particularly interested in developing explainable and robust machine learning models for network security. I am also interested in the intersection of machine learning and cybersecurity, including ai-powered cyber attacks and adversarial machine learning.
