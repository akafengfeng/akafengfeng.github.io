Short bio: I am an AI Security and Safety Researcher at the Artificial Intelligence Institute, China Academy of Information and Communications Technology (CAICT), Beijing. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from the University at Buffalo (SUNY) in 2025, where my advisor was Prof. Hongxin Hu. My doctoral research was supported by the Amazon Research Award. I received my bachelor's degree from Xi'an Jiaotong University.

Research Interests

My research focuses on AI safety benchmarking, Agentic AI security, LLM code security, cybersecurity evaluation of frontier models. I also contribute to AI safety governance and standardization policy research for the National Development and Reform Commission (NDRC), the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology (MIIT), and the National Data Administration(NDA).
